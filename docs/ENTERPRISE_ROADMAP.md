# Enterprise Product Roadmap

## Phase 1 - Secure SaaS MVP
- Login-first private app
- Supabase Auth
- Protected routes
- Professional dashboard
- Core pages for operations

## Phase 2 - Real database and RLS
- Tenant tables
- Organization membership
- Role-based permissions
- Work orders, customers, vessels, invoices, inventory
- Row Level Security policies

## Phase 3 - Revenue features
- Stripe SaaS subscriptions
- Customer invoice payments
- Estimate approval flow
- Automated receipts and reminders

## Phase 4 - Customer and technician portals
- Customer approval/payment portal
- Technician mobile views
- Photo uploads
- Digital signatures
- Time tracking

## Phase 5 - AI workflow engine
- AI estimate draft
- AI damage notes from photos
- AI customer emails
- AI invoice/service summaries
- Predictive inventory alerts
